# Visual Style Guidelines

When generating visual media (images, icons, UI assets), always adhere to the following aesthetic:

- **Palette**: Strictly monochromatic (grayscale or single-tone focus).
- **Composition**: Minimalistic but emotionally charged. Avoid clutter.
- **Lighting**: Dark, dramatic, high-contrast (low-key lighting).
- **Tone**: Powerful irony, poetic justice, and a sense of "humor born of tragedy and utter despair."
- **Connection**: Aim to make the viewer feel like they share an inside joke born of deeply emotional pain shared with the creator.

# Application Context
This application is a Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) dashboard with dual modes:
1. **Defensive Mode**: Analyzing malicious payloads and IOCs.
2. **Offensive Mode**: Simulating cyber campaigns and payload delivery.

Visuals should reflect the cold, clinical nature of high-stakes digital warfare tempered by the creator's philosophical despair.
